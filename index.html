What We Do

We are a young group of web designers, eager to learn as much as we can about html, css, javascript, bootstrap, wordpress, and all the little things that make a website tick. Primarily, our goal of this club is to get experience working together as a group by creating, updating, and launching websites for our fellow groups within the school as well as small non-profit organizations in the area. Through this club, we hope to gain valuable experiences with the programs and tools used for creating websites, and continue to grow the world of the internet through our creativity.
